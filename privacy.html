Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Introduction

Second Brain AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Thoughts and Notes: When you use Second Brain AI to capture thoughts, ideas, tasks, or notes, this data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information unless you explicitly choose to sync it through premium features.

Account Information: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID and optionally your email address and name, as provided by Apple's authentication service.

API Keys (Optional): If you choose to provide your own Google Gemini API key for enhanced AI features, this key is stored securely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, frequency of use, and app performance metrics. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the app experience.

Device Information: We collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of Second Brain AI
  • Process your thoughts and notes using AI classification
  • Enable premium features such as cloud synchronization (if subscribed)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send you notifications about your thoughts and weekly digests (if enabled)
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your thoughts, notes, and personal data are stored locally on your device by default. We do not have access to this information.

Cloud Sync (Premium): If you subscribe to Premium and enable cloud synchronization with Google or Office 365, your data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL). We act as a conduit between your device and these third-party services and do not store your synced data on our servers.

Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Apple Sign In

When you sign in with Apple, we receive limited information as permitted by Apple's authentication service. Apple's Privacy Policy governs their handling of your data.

Google Services (Premium)

If you enable Google synchronization, you authorize us to access your Google Calendar, Contacts, and Tasks on your behalf. Google's Privacy Policy governs their handling of your data.

Office 365 Services (Premium)

If you enable Office 365 synchronization, you authorize us to access your Outlook, OneNote, and Microsoft To Do on your behalf. Microsoft's Privacy Policy governs their handling of your data.

Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI service to classify your thoughts and generate tags. Your thought content is sent to Google's servers for processing. Google's Privacy Policy governs their handling of your data: policies.google.com/privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Export: Export your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Delete: Clear all your data from the app at any time through Settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications, AI features, or cloud sync at any time

Children's Privacy

Second Brain AI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
