Frequently Asked Questions

How do I capture a thought?
Tap the microphone icon to use voice input, or tap the text field to type your thought. The AI will automatically classify and tag it for you.
Is my data private?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device by default. We never access your thoughts unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (Premium feature).
Does the app work offline?
Absolutely! Second Brain AI is designed to work completely offline. All core features including voice input and AI classification work without an internet connection.
What's included in Premium?
Premium includes cloud sync with Google Calendar, Contacts, Tasks, Office 365, cloud backup, cross-device sync, and priority support. It's $2.99/month or $19.99/year (save 44%).
Can I export my data?
Yes! Go to Settings and use the Export feature to download all your thoughts in JSON or CSV format.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone → Tap your name → Subscriptions → Select Second Brain AI → Tap Cancel Subscription.
How accurate is the AI classification?
Our AI is trained to classify thoughts into People, Projects, Ideas, and Admin categories with high accuracy. However, you can always manually adjust classifications if needed.
What languages are supported?
Currently, Second Brain AI supports English. We're working on adding more languages in future updates.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at support@ashanillc.com with details about the bug, including your device model and iOS version. We appreciate your help in making the app better!
